Placebo Studio founded in 2017, the name ‘placebo’ is a metaphor for the process of creating art as the phenomenon, in which the recipient perceives an improvement in condition due to personal expectations rather than the treatment itself. The core members Shandi YC Hsin and JJ Liou are from different background, aim to break the boundary of art forms by the interdisciplinary thinking and explores the possibility of innovation and creativity.
